## Problem 4

**Problem**: Prove that:

### (a)

$$
\begin{aligned}
    & \exists\; e_1 \lt e_2 \lt e_3 \;;\;
    \\
    & W_{e_1} = \{e_2\}
    \\
    & W_{e_2} = \{e_3\}
    \\
    & W_{e_3} = \{e_1\}
\end{aligned}
$$
